About The Early Bird Kit

The Early Bird Kit is a comprehensive learning resource designed to promote early childhood development through the use of flashcards. This kit includes an impressive collection of 200+ flashcards, carefully curated to cover a wide range of subjects and concepts suitable for young learners.


The Early Bird Kit is divided into 5 stages, each tailored to specific age groups and developmental milestones. Starting from Stage 1, which focuses on basic sensory stimulation, all the way to Stage 5, which introduces more advanced cognitive skills, the kit ensures a gradual and progressive learning experience for children.

Learning Resource
200+ Flashcards

Within each stage, the Early Bird Kit offers 10 sets of flashcards, each focusing on a specific area of learning. These sets enable children to explore and master essential concepts in language, numbers, colors, shapes, animals and more. The variety of flashcard sets ensures a well-rounded educational experience.
